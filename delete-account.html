GoFix – Home Services

Account Deletion Request

Delete Account & Data
Last Updated: June 2025

GoFix – Home Services respects your right to delete your account and associated data at any time. This page explains how to request account deletion and what happens to your data after deletion.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your account and all associated data will be permanently removed.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your GoFix account, please contact us through any of the following platforms available in the Contact Us section of the GoFix app:

📸
Instagram
Send a DM on Instagram
📘
Facebook
Send a message on Facebook
📧
Gmail
Send an email

What to Include in Your Request

1
Open the GoFix app and go to Contact Us.
2
Choose any platform — Instagram, Facebook, or Gmail.
3
Send a message with subject or title: "Delete My Account"
4
Include your registered email address so we can identify your account.
5
Our team will process your request and permanently delete your account within 7 business days.

What Data is Deleted

Data Type Status
Full Name Deleted
Email Address Deleted
Phone Number Deleted
Profile Picture Deleted
Location Data Deleted
Booking History Deleted
CNIC Photograph (Technicians only) Retained up to 90 days if under active dispute
Complaint Records Retained up to 90 days for safety purposes

Data Retention Period

  • Most data is deleted immediately upon account deletion.
  • CNIC photographs may be retained for up to 90 days if there is an active dispute or investigation involving the Technician.
  • Complaint records may be retained for up to 90 days for safety and fraud prevention purposes.
  • After this period, all retained data will be permanently deleted.

Contact

For any questions about account deletion or your data, please reach out through the Contact Us section in the GoFix app via Instagram, Facebook, or Gmail.
