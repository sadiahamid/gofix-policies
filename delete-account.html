Account Deletion

📱 How to delete from App:

  1. Open the GoFix App.
  2. Tap the Sidebar Menu (top-left).
  3. Scroll down to the bottom.
  4. Click on Delete Account.
⚠️ Warning

Deleting your account is permanent. All your data, booking history, and profile details will be removed within 7 working days.
