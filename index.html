Privacy Policy
Last Updated: June 2025

GoFix ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you register and use GoFix, we collect:

From all users:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Profile Picture (optional)
  • Location (to match with nearby Technicians)
  • Device information for app functionality
  • Complaints or feedback submitted through the app

From Technicians only:

  • CNIC (National Identity Card) photograph — required for identity verification before account approval

2. CNIC Data – Collection & Use

GoFix collects a photograph of the Technician's CNIC strictly for identity verification purposes.

  • CNIC data is used only to confirm that the Technician is a real, identifiable individual before they are allowed to offer services.
  • CNIC information is stored securely using Firebase and is accessible only to GoFix Admin.
  • CNIC data will NOT be sold, rented, or shared with any third party, except where required by law or in cases of serious dispute or fraud.
  • Technicians may request deletion of their CNIC data by deleting their account. However, in active dispute or investigation cases, data may be retained for a limited period.

3. Commission & Financial Data

GoFix does NOT collect, store, or process any payment or banking information from users.

  • All payments happen directly between Customers and Technicians.
  • After the 3-month free trial, GoFix charges Technicians a 10% commission per completed service. This is a platform usage fee and is separate from any payment made by the Customer.
  • No bank account, card, or wallet information is stored in GoFix.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your GoFix account
  • Verify Technician identity through CNIC
  • Connect Customers with available Technicians nearby
  • Display your profile to the relevant party during bookings
  • Handle complaints and resolve disputes between users
  • Improve the performance and features of the app

5. Data Sharing

GoFix does NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

  • When a service is booked, basic profile information (name, contact) is shared between the Customer and Technician to facilitate the service.
  • In the event of a complaint, relevant user information may be reviewed by the Admin to take appropriate action.
  • CNIC data is strictly accessible to Admin only and is never shared with Customers or other Technicians.

6. Data Security

We use Firebase (Google) as our backend, which applies industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, as a student project, GoFix does not guarantee absolute security and is not liable for any data breaches caused by third-party services.

7. Location Data

GoFix may request access to your device location to help match you with nearby Technicians. Location data is used only within the app and is not shared externally or stored permanently.

8. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Update or correct your profile information at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Request information about what data we hold about you
  • Withdraw consent by discontinuing use of the app

9. Children's Privacy

GoFix is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Student Project Notice

GoFix is an academic student project and is not a registered commercial entity. This Privacy Policy is provided in good faith for educational purposes. We strive to handle all user data responsibly.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app. Continued use of GoFix after an update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the GoFix app support section.
